Dr. Alexandra Lieberman D.O. F.A.C.O.O.G


Dr. Lieberman strives to care for women as if they are our mothers, daughters, and sisters; knowing that each is a treasure to be honored.

She believes in listening and then helping you to make the health care choices that are right for you. You can count on her for quality compassionate healthcare and a natural warmth that makes you feel comfortable.

In addition to her compassion Dr. Lieberman is well trained and experienced. She completed her undergraduate education at The University of Michigan and received her medical degree from NovaSoutheastern College of Osteopathic Medicine.

She completed her residency through Ohio University at St. Vincent’s Mercy Medical Center, a Level I Trauma Campus in Toledo, Ohio. She received specialized training in the areas of obstetrics and gynecology. Dr. Lieberman is a Board Certified Obstetrician/Gynecologist.

Dr. Lieberman’s interests include prevention and holistic medicine, normal and complicated obstetrics and minimally invasive gynecologic surgery. Dr. Lieberman’s family has resided in the South Florida area for close to 20 years. .
