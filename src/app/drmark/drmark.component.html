Dr. Mark B. McCormick, M.D. F.A.C.O.G.


Dr. McCormick has been an Obstetrician and Gynecologist in Boca Raton, Florida for close to twenty-five years.

He has practiced general Obstetrics and Gynecology with a strong interest in abnormal vaginal bleeding. He has done a large number of uterine ablative procedures for abnormal bleeding. This minor surgery is done as an outpatient and the patient is usually able to return to her normal activities the next day. This allows many women to avoid the much more invasive and painful abdominal hysterectomy surgery.

He also has an extensive background in Obstetrics and has delivered thousands of healthy babies.

Dr. McCormick went to Emory University where he graduated Magna Cum Laud in chemistry. His medical training was at University of Miami School of Medicine and his residency was at Jackson Memorial Hospital's prestigious Ob/Gyn program
